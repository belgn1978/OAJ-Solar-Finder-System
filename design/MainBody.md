# Main Body

## Purpose

The main body is the permanent part of the system.

It accepts interchangeable adapters underneath and removable target plates above.

---

## Contains

- Adapter socket
- Distance scale
- Front plate mount
- Rear plate slots
- OAJ branding

---

## Design Goals

- Printable without supports
- Lightweight
- Strong
- Easy assembly
- Modular

---

## Future Features

- Folding plates
- Adjustable pinhole
- Optional locking clip
- Colour inserts
